# Supernatural S16E09: "The Empty Chair" (Quick Workflow Draft)

## Logline
Sam and Dean discover a hidden Men of Letters soul-print journal containing John Winchester's final message, forcing both brothers to confront the one truth he got right before the boundary war closes in.

## Cold Open
- Bunker blackout at 3:07 AM.
- Dean and Sam hear John's voice in an empty library room.
- A false lead points to haunting activity, but EMF and salt fail.
- Hidden panel opens behind a map wall; they find a brass recorder cylinder labeled: "For my boys."

## A Story
- The journal plays in fragments as the episode unfolds.
- John reveals he found boundary references in 2002 and kept silent to protect them.
- Sam is angry at the secrecy; Dean defends John, then cracks when the next message addresses him directly.
- Midpoint: A hostile hunter cell learns the bunker has a "Winchester relic" and attacks.
- Climax: Sam and Dean defend the bunker while listening to John's final segment.
- John's final line: "I got everything wrong except one thing: you two."

## B Story
- Castiel tries to decode the soul-print mechanism and discovers the recording is degrading.
- They only get one full playback before it collapses.

## Myth Arc Tie-in
- John's 2002 notes include coordinates and a Men of Letters symbol matching current boundary fractures.
- Final tag reveals one coordinate matches an abandoned chapel tied to the season endgame.

## Emotional Beats
- Sam hears an apology he wanted his whole life.
- Dean hears permission to stop carrying everyone.
- Both realize legacy is not blood debt; it's what they choose now.

## Tag
- The recorder burns out.
- Dean keeps the brass label in his pocket.
- Sam pins the chapel coordinate to the board: "Next stop."
