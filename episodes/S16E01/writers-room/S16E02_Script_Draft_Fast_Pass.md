# S16E02 "Old Country" — Script Draft (Fast Pass)

## Cold Open
Night in rural Ohio. Jane Jovi and two hunters salt a threshold and wait in silence. The line burns out from beneath, as if consumed. Jane whispers: "That's not a breach. That's a rewrite."

## Act 1
Sam and Dean arrive at the Serbian enclave. Jane shows them relic weapons, hand-drawn ward maps, and a hunting ledger older than Men of Letters archives. Dean is skeptical; Sam is fascinated.

## Act 2
Cas checks angel intelligence channels and finds an unexplained blind zone centered over Eastern Europe. Bobby cross-references a Slavic grimoire and discovers "salt lines" were originally one-way borders, not universal barriers.

## Act 3
Crowley appears at the bunker uninvited, claiming Hell's lower command is panicking. "When Hell is afraid, I pay attention." He confirms demonic movement patterns match the same geography Cas flagged.

## Act 4
Team returns to Jane's town. Jane leads a boundary-vision ritual using ash, iron filings, and reversed salt geometry. The ground pulses like distant footsteps from something vast below. Hunters realize they are standing on a seam, not stable ground.

## Tag
In the aftermath, Dean asks what they just saw. Jane answers: "Your maps have edges. The real world does not." Sam marks the location as Priority Black while Crowley quietly orders Hell to seal all old descent routes.

## Notes Integrated from Fast Writers-Room Pass
- Keep Dean practical and skeptical until visual proof lands.
- Give Sam the research bridge between old-country lore and modern hunting tactics.
- Keep Cas uneasy about systemic angelic blind spots.
- Use Crowley as pressure valve and threat calibrator.
