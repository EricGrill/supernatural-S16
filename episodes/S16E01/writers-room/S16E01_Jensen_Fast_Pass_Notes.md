# S16E01 Jensen Fast Pass Notes

* Current beat lands information clearly, but the exchange is mostly explanatory.
* Add interruption pressure (ghost melody bleeding into motel room audio, EMF spike at the exact moment they map the family lines) so the reveal happens under threat.

## Dean Character-Voice Adjustments (3)

1. Cold open car line should be less generic and more Dean-specific.

* Replace:
  * "You still think this is just a wolf?"
* With:
  * "Tell me again how this is a wolf and not our week getting worse."
* Why: keeps the sarcasm edge and lived-in hunter fatigue without overplaying catchphrase territory.

1. Motel deduction line should carry protective older-brother framing.

* Replace:
  * "Not random. Families tied to the railway board in 1891."
* With:
  * "Not random. It's a bloodline hit list, and we're already late."
* Why: Dean compresses intel into tactical urgency; less lecture, more mission clock.

1. Farmhouse combat beat should show action-over-feelings Dean response to Sam injury.

* Add immediate line after Sam is thrown:
  * "Sam! Walk it off. Stay behind me."
* Why: Dean shows concern through command and positioning, not emotional speech.

## Quick Net Effect

* Keeps the fast-pass structure intact.
* Preserves existing myth arc setup.
* Raises Dean authenticity through sharper tactical, sardonic, protective dialogue behavior.
