SUPERNATURAL
Season 16, Episode 1
"The Long Road Home"
Workflow Test Final (Speed Pass)

TEASER

EXT. KANSAS HIGHWAY - NIGHT

Fog rolls over blacktop. The IMPALA cuts through it, headlights low and mean.
ZEPPELIN on the radio.

DEAN drives. SAM rides shotgun, checking a tablet with old police reports.

SAM
Three counties. Same pattern. Cattle dropped in geometric lines.
No blood, no struggle.

DEAN
So either aliens... or a farmer with OCD and a forklift.

Sam side-eyes him.

SAM
You were dead three weeks ago.

DEAN
And now I’m making jokes. So either Heaven glitched,
or you can be happy your brother’s back.

Dean’s smile fades just a little.

DEAN (CONT’D)
I woke up in that barn. Same one. Hay in my mouth.
No angels. No light show. Just... me.

Sam nods, still processing.

SAM
Jack called it a correction.

DEAN
Yeah. Cosmic bookkeeping. Story of my life.

Dean turns up the radio.

CUT TO TITLE: SUPERNATURAL

ACT ONE

INT. BUNKER - WAR ROOM - DAY

Maps, symbols, coffee. MIRACLE the dog sleeps under the table.

DEAN
I still can’t believe you got a condo.

SAM
Apartment. Temporary.

DEAN
You alphabetized spices, didn’t you?

SAM
...Maybe.

Phone on speaker. JODY MILLS (V.O.).

JODY (V.O.)
Boys, my deputies saw the carcasses. It’s not animal attack.
It’s precision. Surgical.

SAM
Any witnesses?

JODY (V.O.)
One. Old ranch hand. Says he saw a woman drawing circles
in the field with lanterns.

DEAN
Witch.

SAM
Maybe.

Dean closes a lore book. Nothing relevant.

DEAN
Bobby’s stacks got nothing. That’s insulting.

ACT TWO

EXT. NEBRASKA FARMHOUSE - DUSK

Windmill squeaks. Protective charms hang from fence posts,
none recognizable from Men of Letters lore.

SAM and DEAN approach cautiously.

A woman steps out: JANA JOVANOVIC (50s), practical coat,
steady eyes, no fear.

JANA
If you break that line, your teeth crack by sunrise.

Dean stops.

DEAN
Okay. Boundaries. Respect.

SAM
We’re hunters. We’re not here to hurt you.

JANA
Hunters are always here to hurt someone.

She studies Dean.

JANA (CONT’D)
You were dead.

DEAN
Starting to hate how obvious that is.

Jana gestures to symbols carved in oak stakes.

JANA
This is starovjerstvo. Old faith. Older than your books.
I am not summoning. I am sealing.

SAM
Sealing what?

Jana doesn’t answer. She watches the sky.

ACT THREE

INT. MOTEL ROOM - NIGHT

Research montage. Sam on laptop. Dean with whiskey and index cards.

SAM
Nothing in Latin grimoires. Nothing in Enochian.
Cas texted one line: "Some old traditions were hidden from angels."

DEAN
Great. Secret menu magic.

A KNOCK at the door. Jana enters uninvited, places a hand-drawn map.

JANA
There is movement under your maps. Under your categories.
Your new God cannot see it yet.

SAM
Jack can’t see it?

JANA
He can see stars. Not what lives between them.

Dean leans in.

DEAN
Give us the blunt version.

JANA
In 1347, my village called it plague.
It was not plague.
It ate people and left no body for burial.

Beat.

JANA (CONT’D)
It is here again.

ACT FOUR

EXT. DINER - NIGHT

Rain taps neon windows. Dean, Sam, Jana in a booth.
Black coffee all around.

SAM
The cattle pattern — anchors?

JANA
County-wide ward. Temporary.

DEAN
Temporary how?

JANA
Until it learns my name.

Dean exhales. Looks to Sam.

DEAN
So, no apocalypse. Just... whatever this is.

SAM
After everything, maybe this is what hunting is now.
No prophecy. Just people in danger.

Dean puts cash on the table.

DEAN
Then let’s go to work.

EXT. BUNKER GARAGE - LATE NIGHT

Dean and Sam sit on the Impala hood. Quiet.
Miracle curls up near Dean’s boot.

SAM
You good?

DEAN
No.
(beat)
Yeah.
(beat)
Ask me again in fifty years.

Sam smiles.

SAM
Deal.

The brothers stare at the stars.

MUSIC CUE: "Carry On Wayward Son" (instrumental intro).

FADE OUT.

END OF EPISODE
