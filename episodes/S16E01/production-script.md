# Episode 01 Production Script (Fast Draft)

Status: workflow test draft, intentionally rough.

## Episode Meta

* Title: Pilot (Working Title)
* Runtime Target: \~42 min
* Tone: Supernatural procedural with family drama
* Draft Mode: fast pass for pipeline testing

## Cold Open

**EXT. RURAL ROAD - NIGHT**
A black vintage car cuts through fog. A radio preacher fades into static.

**INT. CAR - CONTINUOUS**
Two brothers, older and younger, scan a hand-drawn map with red circles.

OLDER BROTHER
You still think this is just a wolf?

YOUNGER BROTHER
No wolf leaves salt burn marks.

They turn onto a dirt lane toward a dark farmhouse.

CUT TO TITLE.

## Act 1

**INT. COUNTY SHERIFF OFFICE - MORNING**
The brothers pose as federal consultants. The sheriff is tired and skeptical.

Key beats:

* Three disappearances in ten days.
* Witness heard a woman singing in an empty field.
* Victims all crossed the same abandoned bridge before vanishing.

**EXT. ABANDONED BRIDGE - DAY**
EMF meter spikes. Younger brother finds old iron chains under the rail.

OLDER BROTHER
Somebody tried to bind something here.

## Act 2

**INT. TOWN ARCHIVE ROOM - DAY**
Montage of ledgers, microfilm, and bad fluorescent lighting.

They uncover local legend:

* 1891: Traveling singer drowned near the bridge.
* Town covered it up to protect railway funding.
* Ballad says she "calls the guilty by name."

**INT. MOTEL ROOM - NIGHT**
Research wall: photos, strings, timeline.

YOUNGER BROTHER
If it's vengeance, why random targets?

OLDER BROTHER
Maybe not random. Maybe descendants.

## Act 3

**EXT. FARMHOUSE - NIGHT**
Storm rolls in. The brothers salt windows, prep iron rounds.

The ghost voice starts as a melody, then turns to a scream. Lights fail.

Action sequence beats:

* Apparition manifests in hallway mirror.
* Younger brother gets thrown, shoulder injury.
* Older brother traps apparition in a ring of iron chain.
* Sheriff arrives, sees enough to break denial.

## Act 4

**INT. CHURCH BASEMENT - PRE-DAWN**
They perform a burn-and-release ritual using recovered remains.

The spirit appears one final time, now calm.

GHOST (SOFT)
Tell them my name.

She dissipates at sunrise.

**EXT. CEMETERY - MORNING**
New headstone installed with full name and dates.

Tag beats:

* Sheriff gives brothers an unsolved case file from nearby county.
* Younger brother notices same sigil from another hunt.
* Season thread established.

## Production Notes (Quick)

* Locations: sheriff office, bridge, motel, farmhouse, church basement, cemetery.
* Effects: mirror apparition, practical wind/light effects, minimal CG enhancement.
* Stunts: single wire-pull throw, breakaway props in farmhouse.
* Music: folk motif tied to ghost theme.
